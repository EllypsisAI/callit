## FIFA Tournament Assessment

### What We Found
The CLAUDE.md references a "FIFA tournament video" from CREW. However, **extensive searching did not surface a specific FIFA video game tournament video** from the @CREWBOYSS channel. There are a few possible explanations:

1. **The video exists but is not well-indexed.** Smaller channels' individual videos often don't appear in web search results. The video may exist on the channel but not be findable via search engines.
2. **It may be a recent upload.** If posted in late 2025 or early 2026, it may not yet be widely indexed.
3. **The format may be informal.** Rather than a full "FIFA tournament" branded video, it could be a segment within a broader challenge/competition video — e.g., "CREW spiller FIFA mod hinanden" (CREW plays FIFA against each other).

### Recommended Action
**Manually check the @CREWBOYSS YouTube channel** for the FIFA video. Look for:
- Videos with "FIFA" or "EA FC" in the title
- Gaming challenge videos between members
- Any tournament-format competition content

This is the one gap in the research that requires a human to verify by browsing the channel directly.

### If the FIFA Video Exists — Likely Format
Based on CREW's content style, a FIFA tournament would most likely be:
- **Members playing against each other** (2-4+ players, elimination bracket or round-robin)
- **Casual/comedic tone** — not competitive esports, more like friends trash-talking
- **Short format** — probably a single video (10-20 min), not a multi-part series
- **Stakes** — likely forfeit/dare-based ("loser has to do X"), not points-based

## Prediction Opportunities for CallIt

Based on CREW's content patterns, here are the types of predictions fans could make:

### High Confidence (These formats clearly work)
1. **Tournament/competition outcomes** — If CREW does FIFA or other game tournaments, fans predict match winners, tournament champion, biggest upset
2. **Challenge outcomes** — Who wins a challenge? Does the prank work? Who breaks first?
3. **Festival content** — Will they get a certain number of interactions? Will [member] do [embarrassing thing]?

### Medium Confidence (Would need format tweaking)
4. **Prank reactions** — Predict how Lucas reacts to a prank (angry, laughing, confused)
5. **Viewer polls turned predictions** — "Who will [X] happen to first?"
6. **Collab predictions** — When CREW features a guest, predict outcomes of challenges/games

### What Makes CREW Good for CallIt
- **Group dynamic**: Multiple members = multiple outcomes to bet on
- **Competition-friendly content**: Pranks, challenges, and events have natural "winners" and "losers"
- **Engaged young audience**: 16-22 year olds on mobile — exactly the demographic that would use a prediction app for fun
- **Regular uploads**: ~159 videos since August 2023 means consistent content to build prediction markets around
- **Danish niche**: Small enough community that a prediction platform feels personal, not corporate

### What to Watch Out For
- **Irregular schedule**: We couldn't confirm a fixed upload schedule. Prediction markets work best with predictable content drops.
- **Content variety**: Not all videos have clear "predictable" outcomes. Some are just vlogs.
- **Language barrier**: Platform would need to be in Danish for this audience.

## Summary for PoC Planning

CREW is a solid target for the CallIt PoC.
