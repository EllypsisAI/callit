## 5. How Predictions Work

- Events are created by administrators based on content creator activities.
- You place predictions using your calls (points) on possible outcomes.
- Odds are calculated dynamically based on all predictions placed (parimutuel model).
- Results are entered by administrators after events conclude.
- If your prediction is correct, you receive calls (points) based on the odds at the time of resolution.
- If your prediction is incorrect, you lose the calls (points) you placed.
- **All predictions are final.** Once placed, a prediction cannot be changed or withdrawn.
