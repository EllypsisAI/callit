## 2. What Data We Collect

We collect the minimum data needed to run the platform:

| Data | Purpose | Basis (GDPR) |
|------|---------|---------------|
| **Email address** | Account creation, magic link authentication, essential notifications | Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) |
| **Display name** | Shown on leaderboards and predictions | Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) |
| **Prediction activity** | Game functionality (what you predicted, when, outcomes) | Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) -- necessary for the service to function |
| **Basic analytics** | Page views, general usage patterns (aggregated, no tracking across sites) | Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) |

### What we do NOT collect

- Passwords (we use magic link authentication)
- Payment information (there are no payments)
- Location data
- Device fingerprints
- Data from third-party social media accounts
- Sensitive personal data (health, religion, political opinions, etc.)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

1. **Authentication**: Sending magic links to your email so you can log in
2. **Platform functionality**: Displaying your display name, tracking your predictions and point balance
3. **Leaderboards**: Showing rankings based on prediction performance
4. **Service improvements**: Understanding general usage patterns (aggregated data only)

We do not:
- Sell your data to anyone
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for profiling or automated decision-making
- Send marketing emails (unless you explicitly opt in to future updates)

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored on infrastructure located in the EU or with GDPR-adequate safeguards (e.g., Supabase with EU region hosting or equivalent).
- We use HTTPS for all data transmission.
- Magic link authentication means we never store or process passwords.
- We apply reasonable security measures appropriate for a free entertainment platform.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account data** (email, display name): Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
- **Prediction data**: Retained while your account is active. Anonymized or deleted upon account deletion.
- **Analytics data**: Aggregated and anonymized. Not linked to individual accounts.

---

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data (you can update your display name in your account)
- **Erasure ("right to be forgotten")**: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Data portability**: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Object**: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
- **Withdraw consent**: Withdraw consent at any time (by deleting your account)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email to be added]. We will respond within 30 days.

## 7. Cookies

CallIt uses only **essential cookies** required for the platform to function (e.g., session authentication). We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

No cookie consent banner is required for strictly necessary cookies under the ePrivacy Directive. If we ever add non-essential cookies, we will update this policy and implement a consent mechanism.

---

## 8. Children

CallIt requires users to be **13 years of age or older**, in accordance with Denmark's implementation of GDPR Article 8 (Act No. 502 of 23 May 2018, Section 6(2)).

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has created an account, we will delete the account and associated data promptly.
